Featuring a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, garden and views of pool, Rakeen Hotel is located in Amman, 1.9 mi from US Embassy. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi. The hotel features family rooms.


At the hotel, every room comes with a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV. All units at Rakeen Hotel include air conditioning and a desk. Guests at the accommodations can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Rakeen Hotel offers a terrace.


Royal Automobiles Museum is 2.1 mi from the hotel, while Al Hussein National Park is 2.2 mi away. The nearest airport is Queen Alia International, 18 mi from Rakeen Hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
